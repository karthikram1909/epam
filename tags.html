Virat Kohli Profile

Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team.

Career Highlights

Virat Kohli has scored 70 81 Centuries.

He is the fastest batsman to score 12,000 runs in ODI cricket.

Awards and Recognition

Virat Kohli has been awarded the Padma Shri and the Arjuna Award.

He has also been named the ICC ODI Player of the Year in 2012 and 2017.

Records and Achievements

Virat Kohli is the fastest batsman to score 10,000 runs in ODI cricket.

He has also scored the most centuries in ODI cricket as a captain.

Virat Kohli and MS Dhoni are the most successful captain-batsman duo in ODI cricket.

Virat Kohli and AB de Villiers are the most destructive batting duo in IPL history.

Personal Life

Virat Kohli is married to Anushka Sharma.
He is also a fitness enthusiast.
